Account Deletion

Last updated: February 2026

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. We will remove your cloud save, local data, and revoke authentication.

If you have the app installed

Delete your account directly in the app:

  1. Open The Last Siege - Tower Defense
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Under Account, tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion (two-step confirmation)

This permanently deletes your account and all game data.

If you cannot access the app

If you have uninstalled the app, lost your device, or cannot access it for any reason, you can request account deletion by email:

  • Email: tancauschy@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include: your player ID (if you know it), or the email address linked to your account (Google Play Games, Unity account)

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Third-party data

We delete your data from our systems and Unity Gaming Services. Third-party platforms (e.g., Google Play Games) may retain data according to their own policies. You may need to manage those separately.
