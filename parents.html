Information for Parents

Last updated: February 2026

The Last Siege - Tower Defense provides a tower defense gaming experience. This page explains how we protect players and maintain a responsible environment.

1. Safety and Content

Our game focuses on strategy and entertainment. We avoid:

  • Graphic violence
  • Inappropriate language
  • Adult themes
  • Gambling or simulated gambling

Content involves defending against waves of enemies in a stylized tower defense format.

2. Account and Data

  • The Game creates an anonymous account to save progress
  • Optional account linking (Google, Unity) is for recovery only
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13
  • See our Privacy Policy for full details

3. Advertising

The Game displays advertisements. This game is not directed to children under 13. We strive to ensure ads comply with industry guidelines. Ad content is provided by third-party networks and is not fully controlled by us.

4. Data Protection for Minors

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Only anonymous gameplay and analytics data may be collected for players of all ages. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, contact us immediately.

5. Tips for Parents

  • Encourage breaks during gameplay
  • Use device-level parental controls if needed
  • Discuss healthy screen-time habits
  • Review our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions
