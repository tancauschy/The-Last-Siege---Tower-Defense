Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Last Siege - Tower Defense ("the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game and related services. By using the Game, you consent to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous player ID (generated by Unity Gaming Services)
  • Game progress and save data (stored via Unity Cloud Save)
  • Device information (type, operating system, screen size, language)
  • Usage data (play sessions, levels completed, gameplay interactions)
  • IP address and mobile device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID) for analytics and advertising

b) Information You Provide

  • Google account or Unity account (only if you choose to link for account recovery)
  • Any information you send when contacting support

c) Information from Third Parties

When you link accounts (e.g., Google Play Games, Unity Player Accounts), we may receive your third-party account name and profile data as permitted by that platform's privacy policy.

2. Why We Collect Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and save your progress
  • To operate and improve the Game
  • To display advertisements (via Unity Ads / LevelPlay)
  • To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance the experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We and our partners may use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies for analytics, advertising, and functionality. You can control ad personalization via your device settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Save and restore your game progress across devices
  • Provide and improve the Game
  • Display personalized advertisements (this Game is not directed to children under 13)
  • Analyze usage and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests

5. Who Can See Your Data

  • Our platform: We process your data to provide the Game.
  • Partners: Third-party service providers (Unity, LevelPlay, Google) process data on our behalf for hosting, analytics, and advertising. They operate under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose data when required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.

6. Third-Party Services

The Game uses:

  • Unity Gaming Services (authentication, cloud save)
  • Unity Ads / LevelPlay (advertising)
  • Google Play Services (Android distribution, optional account linking)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Advertising

The Game displays personalized advertisements via Unity Ads (LevelPlay mediation). Ad networks may use device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. This Game is not directed to children under 13. You can opt out of personalized ads via device settings.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Game. We periodically review and de-identify unnecessary data. When you delete your account, we delete your data as described in Section 10.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted transmission and secure cloud storage via Unity Gaming Services. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Account > Delete Account)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via device settings)

For GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) users:

  • Access, portability, correction, erasure, restriction, and objection rights may apply
  • Contact us to exercise these rights
  • We will respond within 30 days (or as required by law)

11. Children's Privacy

This Game is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have data from a child under 13, contact us at tancauschy@gmail.com

12. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • In-app: Settings > Account > Delete Account (two-step confirmation)
  • Web: Account deletion page – instructions and email option if you cannot access the app

We will delete your cloud save, local data, and revoke authentication. Third-party platforms (e.g., Google) may retain data per their policies.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes through the Game or at next launch. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

14. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at: tancauschy@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy is designed to align with applicable laws including GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.
